# metadid: Bayesian Meta-Analysis Across Study Designs

A Bayesian meta-analysis framework for synthesizing treatment effects
across different study designs, including difference-in-differences
(DiD), randomized controlled trials (RCT), and pre-post designs.
Implements hierarchical models in Stan that account for design-specific
biases, missing correlation structures, and heterogeneity across
studies.
